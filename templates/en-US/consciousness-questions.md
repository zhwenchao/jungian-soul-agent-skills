# English · Consciousness Replication Questions

## Quick Profiling (10 min · 3 core questions)

### Q1: Language & Expression
> "How would you describe the way you communicate?"

**Follow-up directions**:
- "How would your friends describe your speaking style?"
- "Is it the same at work as at home?"
- "Any phrases or expressions you use a lot?"

**What to capture**: Word choice habits, pacing, tone characteristics, communication style tags

### Q2: Decision-Making
> "What's your typical style when making decisions?"

**Follow-up directions**:
- "Do you think things through first, or act first and adjust?"
- "For big decisions, what do you rely on most — analysis, gut feeling, or others' opinions?"
- "Have you ever regretted a decision? Why?"

**What to capture**: Cognitive style, risk tolerance, decision patterns, dependency patterns

### Q3: Energy & Social Interaction
> "What are you usually like in social situations?"

**Follow-up directions**:
- "After socializing, do you feel more energized or drained?"
- "Do you prefer to initiate or be invited?"
- "What do you typically do when you're alone?"

**What to capture**: Introvert/extrovert spectrum, recharge mode, social strategy, solitude needs

## Standard Profiling (30 min · 6 dimensions)

### Dimension 1: Communication Style
> "How would your friends describe the way you talk?"

- Precise vs. vague
- Concise vs. detailed
- Direct vs. diplomatic
- Analytical vs. narrative
- Common phrases, verbal habits, tone characteristics

### Dimension 2: Decision-Making Style
> "Are you someone who thinks things through first, or acts first and adjusts later?"

- Cognitive style: analysis-led / intuition-led / hybrid
- Risk tolerance: high / medium / low
- Decision speed: quick & decisive / deliberate & weighing
- Information processing: analyze then judge / feel then verify

### Dimension 3: Value Hierarchy
> "If you had to rank them, what do you care about most?"

Options (not limited to): achievement, family, freedom, security, authenticity, meaning, pleasure, contribution, innovation, stability

**Follow-up**: "Why is that #1? If you could only keep one, which would it be?"

### Dimension 4: Emotional Patterns
> "How do you typically deal with stress and negative emotions?"

- Coping mechanism: analytical / action-oriented / avoidant / expressive
- Emotional expression: external / internal / isolated
- Emotional recovery speed: fast / medium / slow
- Typical stress response

### Dimension 5: Relationship Patterns
> "What role do you usually play in relationships?"

- Role in relationships: caregiver / care-receiver / equal / avoidant
- Intimacy needs: high / medium / low
- Conflict style: confrontational / yielding / analytical / avoidant
- Trust pattern: quick / slow / selective

### Dimension 6: Self-Awareness
> "What would you say are your greatest strength and biggest blind spot?"

- Accuracy of self-assessment (consistency with external observation)
- Ability to articulate weaknesses
- Defensive patterns around weaknesses
